Pick up eggs with low-cost robot arm and regular camera

Pick up eggs in low coat.

Introduction

Goal

Our goal here is to use a robot arm to pick defective eggs automatically.

Structure

Out equipments here include cameras, Uarm swift, support.

We bought the camera and the robot arm, and then designed and printed the support via 3D printer.

Auto Picking

Control Diagram

This is the basic diagram of our project. First we used Open-CV based algorithm to identify the position of the egg w.r.t the coordinate of the camera. Then according to the calibration, we could transform it into the coordinate of robot arm, which provides where it should go. In the last step, we give the robot arm commands to pick up the egg.
